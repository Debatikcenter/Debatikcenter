/FIND YOUR TONGUE



Happening by Pleurad Xhafa
July 4, 2023

FIND YOUR TONGUE is an invitation to the visitor, offering them to drink water from ice glasses shaped like the tongue of the former anti-drug chief Dritan Zagani. In 2017, while still on duty, Zagani investigated an Albanian organized criminal group that cooperated with the Albanian government. The former Minister of the Interior of the Socialist Party, Sajmir Tahiri, also was part of it. As a result, today, Tahiri is serving his sentence at his home, after having been previously imprisoned. Zagani was and still is viciously persecuted by the Prime Minister and the Albanian government because according to the Albanian state he was the real criminal. While still continuing to be wanted by the Albanian justice Zagani received political asylum in Switzerland. Nowadays he lives under a new identity, carrying out various jobs, among others as a security guard at Art Basel.
